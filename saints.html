St. Carlo Acutis

St. Carlo Acutis is a modern-day saint who used the internet to spread the Catholic faith. He is known for his devotion to the Eucharist and his efforts to document Eucharistic miracles.

St. Thomas Aquinas

St. Thomas Aquinas is one of the greatest Catholic philosophers and theologians. His work on natural law and the existence of God has influenced Catholic thought for centuries.
