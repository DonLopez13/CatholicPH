Ramil Parba vs. Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano

This theological debate between Ramil Parba, a member of the Iglesia ni Cristo (INC), and Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano centered on key differences in doctrine. Both participants provided compelling arguments, but Rev. Fr. Gitgano's theological expertise and logical clarity earned him a decisive victory.

Through respectful dialogue, the debate demonstrated the importance of understanding faith deeply and defending the Church's teachings with humility and strength.
