Debate Overview

Filipino Debate: This debate features an important exchange of ideas between Ramil Parba, a member of the Iglesia ni Cristo (INC), and Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano, a Catholic priest.

The debate took place in the context of religious differences between the INC and Catholicism, where each side presented arguments defending their beliefs on key theological points. Ramil Parba, representing the INC, argued from his group's perspective, while Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano defended the Catholic position.

Introduction to the Participants

Ramil Parba: A member of the Iglesia ni Cristo, Ramil Parba is known for engaging in public debates about the doctrines of the INC and the Catholic Church.

Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano: A respected Catholic priest, Fr. Darwin Gitgano has a deep understanding of Catholic theology and is known for defending the Church's teachings in debates with various religious groups.

Details of the Debate

The debate focused on several theological points, including the nature of salvation, the role of the Church, and the authority of scripture. Both participants gave passionate and well-prepared speeches, each trying to make a compelling case for their beliefs.

Reflection on the Outcome

After hours of discussion, many observers believed that Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano emerged victorious in this debate. His clear articulation of Catholic teachings, combined with his ability to answer the questions and challenges posed by Parba, gave him the edge in this exchange. Many who attended the debate felt that Fr. Darwin's arguments were more consistent with Catholic doctrine and more convincing overall.
