The Debate Between Ramil Parba and Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano

This debate featured Ramil Parba, a member of the Iglesia ni Cristo (INC), and Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano, a Catholic priest. The discussion revolved around key theological differences between the two faiths.

Both participants presented their arguments passionately. Fr. Darwin Gitgano emerged victorious, showcasing the depth and clarity of Catholic theology in addressing key points raised by the INC.
