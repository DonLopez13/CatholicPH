Introduction

Saint Carlo Acutis was a young Italian Catholic who used the internet to spread devotion to the Eucharist. He is often called the "Cyber Apostle of the Eucharist."

Early Life

Born in 1991, Carlo showed deep faith from a young age. He attended Mass daily, prayed the Rosary, and had a special devotion to the Eucharist.

His Digital Evangelization

Carlo created a website cataloging Eucharistic miracles from around the world. His work continues to inspire people globally.

Beatification and Legacy

Carlo passed away in 2006 due to leukemia but was beatified in 2020. His legacy as a digital evangelist lives on, encouraging young Catholics to use technology for good.
