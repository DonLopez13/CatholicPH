Ramil Parba vs. Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano

The Catholic Church has faced various challenges in the modern era, including debates with other denominations. One notable debate was between INC member Ramil Parba and Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano. This event captivated the attention of many as it highlighted theological arguments from both sides.

Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano, a respected Catholic priest known for his deep understanding of theology, represented the Catholic Church. On the other hand, Ramil Parba, a prominent member of the Iglesia ni Cristo (INC), argued for his beliefs. The debate, which centered around the authenticity of the Catholic Church's teachings, showcased the strength of Catholic doctrines.

In the end, Rev. Fr. Gitgano's well-researched arguments and calm demeanor earned him the victory. This debate serves as a reminder of the importance of defending one's faith with knowledge and humility.
