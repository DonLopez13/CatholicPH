The Debate

In this debate, we will explore the discourse between Ramil Parba, a member of the Iglesia ni Cristo (INC), and Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano, a representative of the Catholic Church. The debate primarily focuses on various doctrinal differences between the two sides.

The debate took place in front of an audience eager to witness the clash of ideas. Ramil Parba presented the INC's position on key theological issues, while Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano defended the teachings of the Catholic Church. The exchange was intense, but many believed that Fr. Darwin had successfully refuted the claims made by Parba.

Filipino Debate Context

This debate is significant in the Filipino context, as it touches on the relationships between different Christian denominations within the Philippines, a nation with a rich Catholic history.

Victory of Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano

While the debate was intense and informative, many observers agreed that Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano emerged victorious, as he was able to effectively address and refute the arguments put forth by the INC member. The clarity and depth of his responses were praised by many viewers and experts.
