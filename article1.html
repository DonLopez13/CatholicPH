Debate Overview

Filipino Debate: This debate features an important exchange of ideas between Ramil Parba, a member of the Iglesia ni Cristo (INC), and Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano, a Catholic priest.

The debate took place in the context of religious differences between the INC and Catholicism, where each side presented arguments defending their beliefs on key theological points. Ramil Parba, representing the INC, argued from his group's perspective, while Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano defended the Catholic position.

Introduction of the Participants

Ramil Parba, a member of the Iglesia ni Cristo, is known for his firm beliefs in his religion's teachings. In contrast, Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano, a Catholic priest, is an advocate for the teachings of the Roman Catholic Church.

The Debate

In this heated debate, Ramil Parba presented arguments that challenged Catholic doctrine, while Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano defended the teachings of the Catholic Church, focusing on key theological differences between the two faiths.

The Victory of Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano

After an intense exchange of ideas, Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano was widely considered to have emerged victorious in the debate. His responses were praised for their clarity, wisdom, and strong defense of Catholic doctrine, while Ramil Parba's arguments were seen as lacking sufficient theological depth.
