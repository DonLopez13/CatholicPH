Introduction

This article discusses a popular debate between Ramil Parba, a member of the Iglesia ni Cristo (INC), and Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano, a Catholic priest. The debate is of significant interest to the Filipino community and has sparked conversations about faith, doctrine, and belief systems.

The Debate

The debate took place in a highly publicized forum. Ramil Parba represented the INC, while Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano defended Catholic teachings. The topic revolved around the nature of Christ and the differences between INC and Catholic views on salvation.

The Victory

After hours of discussion, many people believed that Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano's arguments were more compelling and in line with biblical teachings. His calm and logical approach helped clarify many points of confusion, and he emerged victorious in the eyes of the public.

Filipino Perspective

For many Filipinos, this debate is significant because it touches on the core beliefs that have shaped their cultural and religious identity. The debate has sparked discussions in homes, schools, and churches, especially among those who identify with the INC and those who are faithful to the Catholic Church.
