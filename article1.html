Introduction to the Debate

This debate features INC member Ramil Parba and Catholic priest Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano. The debate revolves around theological and doctrinal differences between the Iglesia ni Cristo (INC) and the Catholic Church.

The Debate

The debate took place in a public forum where both participants presented their views on various issues, such as the nature of the church, salvation, and other key theological points. The audience witnessed a passionate discussion as both speakers presented their arguments.

The Victory of Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano

Despite the challenging nature of the debate, Rev. Fr. Darwin Gitgano's knowledge of Catholic doctrine and his calm demeanor helped him secure the victory. His arguments were well-reasoned, backed by scripture and historical context, leading many to believe that the Catholic position was stronger in this particular debate.
